How it works

Four steps to a language-free call

No earpieces. No interpreters. Just open the app and call.

01

Sign in with your number

Phone OTP only. No email, no password, no friction.

02

Pick your language

Tell Vaani what you speak. It handles the rest automatically.

03

Call a contact

Pick anyone from your phonebook. If they're on Vaani, call instantly.

04

Talk naturally

Each side hears the other in their own language — in real time.

Features

Built for real conversations

Not a demo. An app for the billion people who navigate daily life across language barriers.

Any language pair, zero configuration

Hindi–Tamil, Marathi–English, Gujarati–Telugu — any combination works. Each caller sets their own language once.

⚡

Live transcripts on screen

See what's being said and translated as the call happens — words appear in real time.

📱

Phone number as identity

No usernames to remember. Your contact list is your network — already there.

🔒

Firebase-backed, secure

OTP auth only. Calls are session-scoped and auto-expire after two hours.

📡

Invite via SMS or any app

One tap to send the download link to any contact — SMS, WhatsApp, whatever they use.
